# Health Metro Brand Design System

## Core Identity
Health Metro is a global health connectivity platform dedicated to bridging gaps in healthcare through technology.

**Tagline:** *Connecting Health Globally*

## Visual Language

### 1. Color Palette
The brand uses a high-contrast pairing of deep teal and vibrant orange, representing stability and energy.

### 2. Typography
The brand identity utilizes modern, clean sans-serif typefaces.

*   **Headings:** Bold, blocky sans-serif (similar to *Impact* or *Anton* for the "HM" icon style, but more refined for web).
*   **Body & Tagline:** Geometric sans-serif (e.g., *Inter*, *Montserrat*, or *Outfit*).

### 3. Logo Assets
Located in: [Health Metro Logo](file:///mnt/339c87bd-0631-4f05-b78f-45ed481ff4ca/Health%20Metro/Health%20Metro%20BRAND%20DESIGN%20System/Health%20Metro%20Logo)

*   **Main Logo:** Combined "HM" monogram with a medical cross element in an orange arch.
*   **Medical Cross:** A central white cross within the orange arch, symbolizing health and assistance.

## Design Principles
1.  **Trust & Professionalism:** Use Teal as the dominant color for backgrounds and primary UI elements.
2.  **Energy & Action:** Use Orange for Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, highlights, and important notifications.
3.  **Clarity:** Maintain generous whitespace and high contrast for accessibility.
