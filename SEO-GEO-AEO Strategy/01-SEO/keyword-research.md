# Keyword Research — Health Metro

> A living document. Update monthly after Google Search Console review.

---

## Methodology

We use a 3-layer approach:
1. **Seed Keywords** → Brand + product core terms
2. **Expansion** → Related terms via Ahrefs, Semrush, Google Suggest, PAA
3. **Competitor Gap** → Keywords competitors rank for that we don't

- "health metro bus" / "metro health center" (brand confusion)
