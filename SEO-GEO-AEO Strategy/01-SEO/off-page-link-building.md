# Off-Page SEO & Link Building Strategy — Health Metro

> Domain authority is earned, not bought. This strategy builds lasting authority through relevance and relationships.

---

## Why Links Still Matter

Backlinks from authoritative, relevant domains remain the strongest ranking signal in 2025. For health websites, **relevance + authority + trust** is the winning formula.

**Target Domain Rating (DR):** 35+ within 12 months.

---

## Link Building Tiers

### 🥇 Tier 1 — High-Authority Health & Medical Links (Priority)
These links have the most SEO impact. Pursue aggressively.

| Source Type | Examples | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Health Organizations** | WHO, CDC, NHS, PAHO | Contribute data/reports they can cite |
| **Medical Journals** | PubMed, Lancet, JAMA | Publish research studies, co-author |
| **University Medical Schools** | Johns Hopkins, AIIMS, Imperial London | Partner programs, resource links |
| **Government Health Depts.** | health.gov, nhs.uk | Become listed as a resource/partner |
| **JCI / NABH Accreditors** | jointcommissioninternational.org | Partner listing |

**Outreach Template — Research Partnership:**
```
Subject: Research Collaboration — Global Health Access Data

Hi [Name],

I lead Health Metro (healthmetro.com), a platform connecting [X million] patients with international healthcare.

We've compiled [specific data insight] that may be valuable for your audience at [institution].

Would you be open to a brief collaboration? We're happy to provide our dataset in exchange for co-authorship recognition.

[Signature]
```

### 🥈 Tier 2 — Health Media & Publications
| Publication | Why | How to Get Link |
|---|---|---|
| Medical Tourism Magazine | Direct relevance | Expert contributor / press release |

| Healthline | Massive health DA | Data-driven pitch, expert quotes |
| Modern Healthcare | B2B health audience | Thought leadership piece |
| The Lancet Digital Health | Academic authority | Research collaboration |
| BBC Health / CNN Health | Mass reach | Press release + story angle |

**Digital PR Angles (Linkable Campaigns):**
1. **"Global Health Index"** — Annual data report ranking countries by healthcare quality, cost, and accessibility. Guaranteed media coverage.
2. **"Cost of Healthcare Worldwide"** — Interactive tool/calculator. Gets embedded/linked by financial and health media.
3. **"Medical Tourism Trends Report"** — Annual survey of international patients. Journalists cite these regularly.

## Broken Link Building

1. Use Ahrefs to find broken links on health/medical tourism sites
2. Contact site owners with a replacement link (our content)
3. Focus on pages about medical tourism, international healthcare, health travel

---

## HARO / Expert Positioning

**Tools:** HARO (Help a Reporter Out), Qwoted, SourceBottle

- Monitor daily for queries mentioning: healthcare, medical tourism, global health, digital health, telemedicine
- Respond within 2 hours with expert commentary
- Include: credentials, Health Metro affiliation, website URL
- Target: 5–10 successful placements per month

## Competitor Backlink Gap

**Process:**
1. Get competitor backlink profiles from Ahrefs (Patients Beyond Borders, Bookimed)
2. Filter for: DR 40+, health/travel niche, "do-follow" links
3. Reach out to same sites with a better resource offer

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Buy links (Google penalty risk — will destroy rankings)
- ❌ Participate in link schemes / PBNs
- ❌ Use exact-match anchor text excessively
- ❌ Guest post on non-relevant, low-quality sites purely for links
- ❌ Automated link building tools
