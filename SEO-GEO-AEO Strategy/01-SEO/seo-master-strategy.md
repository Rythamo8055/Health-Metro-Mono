# SEO Master Strategy — Health Metro

> **Goal:** Dominate organic search for health connectivity, medical tourism, and global healthcare keywords.

---

## 1. Strategic Overview

Health Metro sits at the intersection of **healthcare** and **technology connectivity** — two of the highest-value search verticals. Our SEO strategy is built on three pillars:

1. **Topical Authority** — Own the "global health connectivity" topic cluster completely
2. **Technical Excellence** — Near-perfect Core Web Vitals and crawlability
3. **E-E-A-T Dominance** — Demonstrate Experience, Expertise, Authority, and Trust at every page

## 4. Site Architecture & URL Strategy

```
healthmetro.com/
├── /                               (Home — brand, global value prop)
├── /about                          (E-E-A-T: team, mission, trust signals)
├── /solutions/
│   ├── /solutions/for-patients     (patient audience hub)
│   ├── /solutions/for-providers    (provider audience hub)
│   └── /solutions/for-insurers     (insurer audience hub)
├── /directory/                     (hospital/clinic directory — SEO engine)
│   ├── /directory/[country]/
│   └── /directory/[country]/[city]/
├── /blog/                          (Topical authority content hub)
│   ├── /blog/medical-tourism/
│   ├── /blog/global-health/
│   └── /blog/healthcare-technology/
├── /resources/
│   ├── /resources/faq              (AEO — FAQ pages)
│   └── /resources/guides           (Long-form pillar pages)
└── /contact
```

### URL Rules
- All URLs: lowercase, hyphen-separated, no trailing slash
- Max 3 directory levels deep
- Include primary keyword in URL where natural

## 5. Content SEO Framework

### Pillar Page Strategy (Hub & Spoke)
Each pillar page targets a broad topic and links to supporting cluster pages.

| Pillar Page | Supporting Cluster Pages |
|---|---|
| "The Complete Guide to Medical Tourism" | Cost by country, Hospital ratings, Insurance coverage, Legal rights |
| "Global Healthcare Connectivity Explained" | Telemedicine, Cross-border care, Digital health records |
| "Finding International Healthcare Providers" | By specialty, By country, By accreditation |

### Content Quality Standards (EEAT)
- **Experience:** Include real patient stories, provider testimonials
- **Expertise:** Author bios with credentials on all health content
- **Authority:** Citations from WHO, NHS, CDC, peer-reviewed journals
- **Trust:** HTTPS, clear privacy policy, contact page, medical disclaimer
