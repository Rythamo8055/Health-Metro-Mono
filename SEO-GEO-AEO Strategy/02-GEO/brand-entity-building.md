# Brand Entity Building — Health Metro

> Establish Health Metro as a recognized, authoritative entity across the knowledge graph so that AI systems understand and cite it correctly.

---

## What Is a "Brand Entity"?

In the context of AI and search engines, an **entity** is a uniquely identifiable concept — a person, place, organization, or thing — that exists in a structured knowledge system (Google's Knowledge Graph, Wikidata, etc.).

When AI systems are asked about a topic, they reason through entities and their relationships. **Health Metro must be a clearly defined entity in as many systems as possible.**

## Brand Descriptor Consistency

Across every platform, use this **canonical brand description:**

**Short (< 100 chars):**
> *Health Metro — Connecting Health Globally. The platform for international healthcare connectivity.*

**Medium (< 250 chars):**
> *Health Metro is the world's leading platform for global healthcare connectivity, connecting patients with accredited international providers in over 50 countries. Tagline: Connecting Health Globally.*

**Long (for About pages, Wikipedia, LinkedIn):**
> *Health Metro is a global health technology platform dedicated to connecting patients with accredited international healthcare providers worldwide. Through its digital platform, Health Metro enables patients to discover, compare, and access medical services across borders, making quality healthcare more accessible regardless of geography. Health Metro's mission is encapsulated in its tagline: "Connecting Health Globally."*

## NAP Consistency (Name, Address, Phone)

Ensure these are **identical** across every directory, platform, and citation:

```
Name: Health Metro
URL: https://healthmetro.com
Phone: [your phone]
Address: [your registered address]
Email: hello@healthmetro.com
Founded: [year]
Category: Healthcare Technology / Medical Tourism Platform
```

**Consistency is critical** — even minor variations (HealthMetro vs. Health Metro vs. Healthmetro) create disambiguation problems for AI systems.
