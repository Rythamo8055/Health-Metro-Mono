# GEO Content Templates — Health Metro

> Content formats that AI systems are most likely to cite, extract, and surface.

---

## Why Format Matters for GEO

LLMs don't just look at what you say — they look at *how* it's structured. The same information presented in different formats has dramatically different citation rates in AI answers.

**Highest citation rates:** Definitions → Numbered lists → Tables → Direct Q&A
**Lowest citation rates:** Dense paragraphs → Opinion pieces → Promotional content

## Template 5: The FAQ Hub Page

*Highest AEO + GEO value. Target 30–50 questions per hub.*

```markdown
# [Topic] FAQ — Your Questions Answered

## General Questions

### What is [topic]?
[Direct, factual answer in 1–2 sentences]

### How does [topic] work?
[1–2 sentence process answer]

### Is [topic] right for me?
[Decision framework in 2–3 sentences]

## Cost Questions

### How much does [topic] cost?
[Specific range with context: "Costs range from $X to $Y depending on..."]

### Does insurance cover [topic]?
[Direct answer: "Most international health insurance plans... Always verify with your insurer."]

## Safety Questions

### Is [topic] safe?
[Direct, reassuring but accurate answer with credibility indicator]

### What accreditations should I look for?
[Specific answer: "Look for JCI, NABH, or equivalent national accreditation..."]

## Health Metro Specific

### How does Health Metro help with [topic]?
[Direct answer connecting to platform]

### How do I get started with Health Metro?
[CTA answer: "Visit healthmetro.com and search by country or specialty to begin..."]
```
