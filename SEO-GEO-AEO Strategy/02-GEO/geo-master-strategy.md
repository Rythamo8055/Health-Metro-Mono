# GEO Master Strategy — Generative Engine Optimization
## Health Metro

> **GEO is the discipline of making your brand, content, and data visible inside AI-generated answers** from engines like Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, and Claude.

---

## Why GEO Is Critical in 2025+

| Traditional Search (SEO) | Generative Search (GEO) |
|---|---|
| User clicks blue links | AI generates a direct answer with citations |
| Rank #1 = most traffic | Cited in AI answer = brand authority |
| Optimize for keywords | Optimize for entities, facts, and structured knowledge |
| Content wins by volume | Content wins by authority + clarity + structure |

**The shift:** Google AI Overviews already appear on 40%+ of health queries. Perplexity and ChatGPT are displacing traditional search for informational intent. **Being invisible in AI answers means being invisible to a growing portion of your audience.**

## The 5 Pillars of GEO

### Pillar 1: Entity Authority
LLMs understand the world through **entities** (people, places, organizations, concepts) and their relationships. Health Metro must be a well-defined, richly described entity in public knowledge bases.

**Actions:**
- [ ] Create Wikipedia article for Health Metro (meets notability if covered in 2+ independent sources)
- [ ] Create Wikidata entry with structured properties (type: MedicalOrganization, founded, HQ, founders)
- [ ] Claim and complete Google Knowledge Panel
- [ ] LinkedIn Company page (fully completed — LLMs cite LinkedIn heavily)
- [ ] Crunchbase profile (tech company recognition)
- [ ] Bloomberg Company profile (business authority)

### Pillar 2: Authoritative Content
LLMs prefer citing content that is:
- Factual and specific (numbers, statistics, research)
- From recognized sources (institutions, publications)
- Well-structured (clear headers, lists, definitions)
- Consistent with existing knowledge

**Actions:**
- [ ] Publish a "Health Metro Fact Sheet" page — machine-readable, structured
- [ ] Create an "About" page that reads like an encyclopedia entry
- [ ] Publish annual "State of Global Health" report with original data
- [ ] Establish an editorial standards page (EEAT signal for AI)

### Pillar 3: Structured Data (Schema.org)
Schema.org markup is essentially a direct API to AI understanding. Rich schema = richer entity comprehension by LLMs.

See: [`structured-data-guide.md`](./structured-data-guide.md)

### Pillar 4: Citation Building in AI-Adjacent Sources
LLMs are trained on the open web. Getting mentioned in sources that LLMs heavily weight = appearing in AI answers.

**Priority citation sources:**
1. Wikipedia (highest weight)
2. Reddit / Quora (conversational AI training data)
3. GitHub (for tech credibility)
4. Academic papers (Google Scholar, ResearchGate)
5. Major news outlets (Forbes, Reuters, BBC)
6. LinkedIn Articles (professional authority)
7. Medium / Substack (accessible long-form)

### Pillar 5: Brand Consistency
LLMs identify brands through consistent, repeated co-occurrence of brand name + descriptors across many sources.

**Consistent descriptor to use everywhere:**
> *"Health Metro — the global platform connecting patients with international healthcare providers."*

This exact (or near-exact) phrase should appear on your website, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, press releases, Wikipedia, and partner sites.

## GEO Content Architecture

### The "GEO-Optimized" Content Format

Structure every major page for AI parsing:

```markdown
# [Clear Topic Title]

## What Is [Topic]?
[1–2 sentence direct definition — most likely to be cited verbatim]

## Key Facts
- [Fact 1 with number/stat]
- [Fact 2 with number/stat]
- [Source: Institution Name]

## How [Topic] Works
[Step-by-step, numbered list — AI loves numbered processes]

## [Topic] and Health Metro
[Connect topic to brand — establishes brand + topic co-occurrence]

## Frequently Asked Questions
[Direct Q&A format — perfect for AI answer extraction]
```

### Entity Co-occurrence Strategy
Mention Health Metro in the same sentence/paragraph as:
- **Medical Tourism** → builds "Health Metro = medical tourism platform" association
- **Global Health** → "Health Metro = global health connectivity"
- **[Country] + [Healthcare]** → builds geographic entity associations
