# Featured Snippet Guide — How to Win Position Zero
## Health Metro

> Featured snippets appear above all organic results. They drive 8% higher CTR than #1 ranked results and are directly cited by Google AI Overviews.

---

## Types of Featured Snippets & How to Win Each

### 1. Paragraph Snippet (Most Common)
**Triggered by:** "What is...", "Why is...", "How does..."
**Format:** 40–60 word paragraph, clear and factual

**How to win:**
```markdown
## What Is Medical Tourism?

Medical tourism is the practice of traveling to another country to receive 
medical care, typically to access higher quality treatment, lower costs, 
or shorter waiting times than available locally. An estimated 14 million 
people travel internationally for healthcare each year.
```

**Rules:**
- Answer must be in the FIRST `<p>` tag after the `<h2>` that mirrors the question
- 40–60 words — short enough to display fully
- Include the target keyword phrase within the answer naturally

---

### 2. List Snippet — Numbered (How-To, Steps)
**Triggered by:** "How to...", "Steps to...", "Ways to..."
**Format:** Numbered `<ol>` with bold step titles

**How to win:**
```markdown
## How to Find a Hospital Abroad

1. **Define your medical need** — Know your diagnosis and treatment required
2. **Research accredited hospitals** — Filter by JCI or equivalent certification  
3. **Compare costs and reviews** — Request treatment quotes from 3+ hospitals
4. **Verify doctor credentials** — Confirm specialist qualifications independently
5. **Plan logistics** — Arrange travel, accommodation, visa, and insurance

[Body content continues below]
```

**Rules:**
- Steps must use `<ol>` (numbered list), not `<ul>`
- Bold the step name before the colon
- Keep each step to 1 line (Google truncates long items)
- 4–10 steps optimal

---

### 3. List Snippet — Bulleted (Best of, Types of)
**Triggered by:** "Best...", "Types of...", "Examples of..."
**Format:** `<ul>` with brief descriptive items

**How to win:**
```markdown
## Best Countries for Medical Tourism

- **Thailand** — World-class hospitals with 40% lower costs than the US
- **India** — Leading cardiology, orthopedics, and oncology at 80% savings
- **Mexico** — Ideal for dental, cosmetic, and weight-loss surgery
- **Turkey** — Rising quality with hair transplants and cosmetic procedures
- **South Korea** — Excellence in cosmetic surgery and cancer treatment

[Full analysis below]
```

---

### 4. Table Snippet
**Triggered by:** Comparison queries, "X vs Y", cost by country queries
**Format:** `<table>` with clear headers

**How to win:**
```markdown
## Medical Surgery Costs by Country

| Procedure | USA | India | Thailand | Mexico |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Heart Bypass | $123,000 | $7,900 | $11,000 | $25,000 |
| Hip Replacement | $40,000 | $7,000 | $12,000 | $13,000 |
| Dental Implant | $4,000 | $900 | $1,200 | $1,100 |
| IVF Treatment | $15,000 | $3,500 | $4,500 | $5,000 |

*Source: International Federation of Health Plans, 2024*
```

**Rules:**
- Keep tables to 4–5 columns max
- First column should be the item being compared
- Include source attribution
- Place table in the first content section of the page

## The Snippet Stealing Strategy

**When a competitor owns a snippet:**

1. Identify the snippet URL and content in Ahrefs → Organic Keywords → filter "Featured snippet"
2. Analyze their answer: word count, format, paragraph vs. list
3. Write a **better answer** — more specific, more accurate, slightly different format
4. Ensure your page ranks in the top 5 for that query (prerequisite)
5. Wait 2–6 weeks for Google to re-evaluate

**Checklist for "better answer":**
- [ ] More specific (includes numbers, countries, sources)
- [ ] Correct format for the query type
- [ ] Page has better overall content quality
- [ ] H2 mirrors the query exactly
- [ ] Answer is in first `<p>` under H2

---

## Featured Snippet Don'ts

- ❌ Don't bury the answer in the middle of a long paragraph
- ❌ Don't start the answer with "I" or "We" (Google doesn't feature first-person)
- ❌ Don't write promotional content — must be factual and neutral in tone
- ❌ Don't exceed 80 words per paragraph snippet (Google shows about 300 chars)
- ❌ Don't rely on snippets for brand queries (Google rarely features these)
