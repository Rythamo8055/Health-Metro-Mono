# PAA Strategy — People Also Ask Domination
## Health Metro

> **PAA (People Also Ask)** boxes appear in 80%+ of Google health queries. Dominating them builds visibility, authority, and drives significant click-through.

---

## Why PAA Matters

- PAA boxes appear on 80%+ of health-related searches
- Each PAA box can contain 4–10 questions — one click reveals 4 more
- Owning PAA answers means your content appears **multiple times on one SERP**
- PAA answers are pulled from featured snippets — optimize for one, win both
- AI Overviews and Google SGE pull heavily from PAA-winning pages

---

## How Google Selects PAA Answers

1. Page already ranks in top 20 for the parent query
2. Content contains an exact (or near-exact) match of the question in a heading
3. The answer is immediately below the heading (not buried)
4. Content is factual, credible, and from an authoritative source
5. FAQPage schema is deployed (boosts eligibility)

## PAA Answer Template

Use this for every PAA answer you write:

```markdown
### [Exact PAA Question as Asked?]

[Direct, factual answer — 40–60 words. Start with the answer, not with "It depends" or "That's a great question."]

[Optional: One follow-up sentence with a supporting stat or source]
```

**Example:**
```markdown
### Is surgery abroad safe?

Yes, surgery abroad is safe when you choose internationally accredited hospitals 
verified by bodies like JCI (Joint Commission International) or NABH. Millions 
of patients travel for medical care annually with high satisfaction rates. 
Always verify accreditation and surgeon credentials before booking.
```

## PAA Cluster Expansion Strategy

PAA questions are connected — answering one reveals 4 more. Map the clusters:

```
"medical tourism" →
  ├── What is medical tourism?
  │     ├── What are the benefits of medical tourism?
  │     │     └── Which country has the best medical tourism?
  │     └── What are the risks of medical tourism?
  │           └── How do I choose a safe hospital abroad?
  └── How much does medical tourism cost?
        ├── Is medical treatment cheaper in India?
        └── Does insurance cover medical tourism?
```

**Strategy:** Build content hubs that answer the entire cluster — not just individual questions. A single hub page answering 10–15 related PAA questions will capture multiple PAA appearances simultaneously.
