# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Highly professional doctors (General Practitioners, Specialists, Surgeons). They are busy, value efficiency, and expect a clinical, authoritative experience that respects their expertise.

## Product Purpose
To onboard medical professionals to the Health Metro network with zero friction, absolute security, and a professional "Clinical Sanctuary" aesthetic.

## Brand Personality
Precise, Clinical, Authoritative, Elegant. 

## Anti-references
- "Startup-y" SaaS websites with generic character illustrations.
- Overly colorful or "fun" consumer apps.
- Cluttered medical portals from the early 2000s.
- Gradients and "AI-slop" aesthetic.

## Design Principles
1. **Clinical Precision**: Every field and label must be exact. No fluff.
2. **The 60-30-10 Rule**: Strict adherence to the brand palette (Teal/Orange) without gradients or decorative fluff.
3. **Expert Efficiency**: Optimized for speed, especially on mobile devices.
4. **Authoritative Elegance**: Use high-fidelity medical illustrations to reinforce trust, but keep the UI minimal.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- High contrast for medical environments.
- Large touch targets for mobile-first clinical use.
