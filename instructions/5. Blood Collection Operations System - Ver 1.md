__2\. HOME COLLECTION ROUTE OPTIMIZATION SYSTEM__

__2\.1 OBJECTIVE__

Optimize field agent routes to:

- Reduce travel time
- Minimize fuel cost
- Increase daily sample collection capacity
- Ensure time slot compliance

__2\.2 INPUT DATA__

Each booking contains:

- Latitude / Longitude
- Time slot
- Priority \(urgent/normal\)
- Customer availability window
- Collection type = Home

__2\.3 ROUTE GROUPING LOGIC__

System groups bookings by:

__A\. Geography Clustering__

- Cluster customers within 2–5 km radius
- Use K\-means or DBSCAN clustering

__B\. Time Slot Grouping__

- Morning route \(7 AM – 11 AM\)
- Afternoon route \(11 AM – 3 PM\)
- Evening route \(3 PM – 7 PM\)

__C\. Priority Rules__

1. Fasting blood tests \(highest priority\)
2. Time\-sensitive samples
3. Normal bookings

__2\.4 ROUTE GENERATION ENGINE__

__Output per agent:__

__Example Route Plan:__

Agent: Ramesh

Route:

1. Stop A – 08:00 AM
2. Stop B – 08:30 AM
3. Stop C – 09:00 AM
4. Stop D – 09:30 AM

__Optimization Logic:__

- Shortest distance path \(TSP approximation\)
- Time window constraint respected
- Avoid backtracking
- Real\-time traffic adjustment \(optional Google Maps API\)

__2\.8 BUSINESS RULES__
