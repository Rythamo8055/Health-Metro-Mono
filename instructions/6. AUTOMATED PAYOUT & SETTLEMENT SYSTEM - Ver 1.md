__4\. REVENUE FLOW ARCHITECTURE__

__A\. HOSPITAL FLOW \(B2B\)__

1. Customer books test under hospital
2. Revenue is accumulated
3. Monthly invoice generated
4. Hospital pays manually

__System Output:__

- Invoice PDF
- Monthly statement
- No automatic payout

__B\. NON\-HOSPITAL FLOW \(AUTOMATED REFERRAL PAYOUT\)__

1. Customer scans QR
2. Booking created
3. Revenue captured
4. Referral payout triggered automatically
